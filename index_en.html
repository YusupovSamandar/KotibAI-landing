Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



Kotib AI is an AI-powered service that helps with text and audio processing. It provides audio and video transcription, translations between Uzbek, Russian, and English, as well as text-to-speech conversion and transliteration from Uzbek Cyrillic to Latin and vice versa. In addition, it can check text for spelling and grammar errors. The resulting text can be used as a short summary or a longer piece of material.

The platform supports the Uzbek, Russian, and English languages, which need to be selected prior to audio and video processing. For audio, MP3, MPEG, WAV, OGG, and M4A are all supported. For videos, MP4, WEBM, and direct links to YouTube videos are all supported as well.

Transcription results are provided in Doc document format, with the option to request a brief summary of the main points or to convert it into an article.

After transcription, you can request to have the transcript converted into an article or other formats, such as a report, interview, or news story. When choosing "Article" service, you can select the genre you want the material to be written in.

Audio and video transcriptions cost 300 soums per minute, while converting text to audio is 5 soums per 10 characters. The cost of translation, transliteration, and error checking varies depending on the length of the text. When you activate the KotibAI telegram bot, you will get a free trial with 16 minutes and 40 seconds.

Payment is available via the following payment systems: Humo, Uzcard, and Click and Pay. When choosing a payment method, you will receive detailed instructions on how to proceed.

Your data is protected. We follow strict data security and privacy policies to safeguard our users' information.

The processing time varies depending on the size of the text. However, most tasks are completed promptly (approximately one minute for video transcription), thanks to the power of artificial intelligence.

Background noise may affect the accuracy of the transcription. We advise minimizing it before uploading the file. Nevertheless, our platform is designed to work with varying audio conditions and provides recommendations to improve the quality of the transcript.

For large projects that involve transcribing hundreds of hours of audio or video recordings, we offer wholesale discounts. Please contact our corporate team for more information.

While 100% accuracy may not be achievable for any automated service, Kotib AI provides one of the most accurate solutions on the market for the Uzbek language, thanks to its AI algorithms.
